What is the Big 5?

The Big 5 is an academically and scientifically supported frmaework for supporting personality that posits that all human personality is some amalgamation/linear combination of these five core dimensions:

  1. Openness/Intellect:
    subsumes intellectual curiosity, abstract thinking, artistic interests, and cultural refinement
  2. Conscientiousness:
    subsumes discipline, prudence, industriousness, and executive function
  3. Extraversion:
    subsumes charisma, expressiveness, willingness to meet new people, and readiness to take action
  4. Agreeableness:
    subsumes politeness, empathy, and willingness to acquiesce to others' needs
  5. Emotional Stability:
    subsumes placidity, patience, levelheadedness, and an overall optimistic attitude on life

Using this website's tools (both AI-powered and in the form of short tests/inventories), you can evaluate both your and other fictional characters' Big 5 personality traits. Have fun!
